Welcome To The Admin Panel

Restaurant

Here you can manage restaurant settings, view performance analytics, and oversee overall operations. You can update business details, opening hours, and policies.

Orders

Track and manage customer orders in real-time. You can update the status of each order, process refunds, and analyze order trends to optimize service.

Menu

Modify the restaurant's menu by adding, editing, or removing items. You can set pricing, manage availability, and create special offers for customers.

Riders

Assign delivery tasks to riders, track their location, and monitor delivery progress. Ensure that deliveries are completed on time and manage rider availability.

Customers

View and manage customer profiles, including order history, preferences, and feedback. Use this data to personalize promotions and improve customer satisfaction.
